padm0nk — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-11

padm0nk is a browser extension that lets you play cloud games with a mouse and keyboard by emulating a game controller inside the page. This policy explains exactly what data it touches.

Data we collect

None. padm0nk does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no remote servers. The extension makes no network requests of its own.

Data we store (on your device only)

padm0nk saves your settings — key bindings, mouse sensitivity, and toggle preferences — using the browser's built-in storage:

  • chrome.storage.local — your active configuration, kept on this device for instant updates.
  • chrome.storage.sync — a backup copy that your browser may sync across your own signed-in devices through your browser account. This is handled entirely by the browser; padm0nk never sees or controls that sync.

This data never leaves your browser to any server operated by padm0nk or its author.

Remote code

padm0nk runs only the code shipped inside the extension package. It does not download, inject, or execute any remote or third-party code.

Permissions

The storage permission is used solely to save the settings described above. The extension runs on Xbox Cloud Gaming (xbox.com/play) and a couple of gamepad-test sites only to provide and verify the emulated controller.
